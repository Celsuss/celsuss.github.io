  • Halcyon Days
    2017

    Halcyon Days is a third person adventure puzzle game where the player must explore in a black and white, bleak and abstract world. As the player you have no idea about why you are there and the only thing you has to rely on is is a bright sphere as the follow. In their search of the light, the player will then encounter challenges in the form of logical puzzles where the player must master two time-manipulation abilities in order to get to end.
    This was my second big project at University, a 10 week group assignment with other of the game dev disciplines/programs from the same year at our school the University of Skövde participating. We were divided into groups with the assignment to create a game together. Our group worked at least 8 hours a day inside a computer lab in our school where we got access to Unity Pro which we used in our work. This was my second game done with Unity but it was my first time ever to use unit testing.
    Language: C# Engine: Unity Pro Team: 5 programmers, 3 designer, 4 audio engineers, 2 game writers, 1 animator, 1D artist and 2 2D artists.

  • Cimmerian Shade
    2017

    Cimmerian Shade is a platform puzzle game where the player can control two characters, a girl and her younger brother. They embark on an adventure to find a friend who mysteriously disappeared, a journey that takes them through a range of different environments each with new obstacles and puzzles to solve in order to finally find out what really happened to their friend. This was my first big project at University, a 10 week group assignment with all of the game dev disciplines/programs in my year at University of Skövde participating. We were divided into smaller groups with the mission to create a game together based on the group designers game idea. Our group worked 8 hours a day (well, maybe a bit more some days… or most days…) at our temporary office (it counts as an office if there is a coffee machine in the room!) in one of the computer labs at Uni. My work on this project included programming the editor we created to make the game as well as many gameplay mechanics (and a whole lot of pest control..). Language: C++ Libraries: SFML 2.1, Box2D Team: 3 programmers, 1 designer, 2 audio engineers, 2 game writers, 1 animator and 1 graphics artist
